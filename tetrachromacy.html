Tetrachromacy on High Refresh Display

This is a simple webpage to simulate red Tetrachromacy on one display. Instead of showing two seperate images in a VR headset this site utilizes the high refresh rate of modern monitors, alternating images at a high frequency. While colors merge on a 60Hz screen, they are not stable. A 120Hz screen or faster is recommended. Use the slider to find a stable FPS for your display.

Images are from the website: Color in Color

Refresh: 120
