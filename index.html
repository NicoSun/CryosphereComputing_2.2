CryosphereComputing

by Nico Sun

The Website is dedicated to monitoring and analysing changes in the polar regions. Daily updated products include Sea Ice Area, Snow Cover, High Arctic Temperature data and a quantification of ocean warming from reduced sea ice (AWP).

Read more

Weekly updated are Antarctic Iceberg locations, speed and size. The Arctic also gets monthly Sea Ice Thickness updates with the AMSR2 product. The Analysis section covers long term trends, extremes and special visualizations.

For Earth's ice sheets current products include a thickness distribution, bedrock elevation overlayed on a true colorimage and a size comparison between glaciers.
